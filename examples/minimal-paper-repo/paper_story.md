# Paper Story

## Thesis

ToyCalib demonstrates, in a minimal controlled setting, that a small entropy-conditioned calibration head can reduce calibration error without materially changing accuracy.

## Gap

The demo focuses on the engineering workflow for evidence-backed paper drafting, not on establishing a new calibration method.

## Contribution Boundary

- Supported: ToyCalib reduces ECE from 0.071 to 0.039 on the included toy result table.
- Careful: Accuracy changes only slightly from 0.842 to 0.845.
- Avoid: state-of-the-art, robustness, real benchmark generalization, or broad deployment claims.

## Target Reader

Researchers evaluating how the skill maps code, notes, and results into paper artifacts.
