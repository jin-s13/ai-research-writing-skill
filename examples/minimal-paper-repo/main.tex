\section{Introduction}

Modern classifiers can be accurate while remaining poorly calibrated. This demo studies a lightweight calibration head for a toy classifier and records the evidence needed before making paper-style claims.

\section{Method}

ToyCalib freezes the classifier backbone and learns a small head over validation logits. The head conditions corrections on logit entropy.
