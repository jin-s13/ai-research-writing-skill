# Literature Positioning

This project sits between five neighboring categories:

1. General research-writing skill collections.
2. AI research skill ecosystems.
3. Nature/CNS-style publication workflows.
4. Publication figure-generation resources.
5. Agent platform packaging conventions.

The closest open-source references used in this example are:

- `Master-cai/Research-Paper-Writing-Skills`: compact research-paper writing skills for ML/CV/NLP contexts.
- `Norman-bury/research-writing-skill`: broad multi-platform research-writing assistant.
- `Orchestra-Research/AI-research-SKILLs`: wider research and engineering skill library.
- `Yuan1z0825/nature-skills`: Nature-style academic writing and publication workflows.
- `ChenLiu-1996/figures4papers`: high-quality figure scripts and examples for papers.

AI Research Writing Skill differs by treating the repository itself as the working substrate. It expects agents to inspect code, logs, notes, templates, citations, and generated assets, then produce a paper package with visible evidence boundaries.
